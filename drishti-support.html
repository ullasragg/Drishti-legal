Frequently Asked Questions

My reading didn't generate — what happened?

This is usually a temporary connection issue. Please check your internet connection and try again. If the problem continues, contact us with the tradition name and roughly when it happened.

I purchased credits but they didn't appear in the app.

Try tapping "Restore Previous Purchases" on the credit pack screen. If credits still don't appear, contact us with your purchase receipt (found in your Apple ID purchase history) and we'll resolve it.

How do I switch the app language?

Go to Settings within the app and tap either "English" or "हिंदी" to switch. Your reading history stays the same — only new readings will be generated in the selected language.

How do I delete my reading history?

Go to Settings → Clear All Reading History. This permanently removes all readings stored on your device.

Are readings stored anywhere besides my device?

No. Reading content is generated on-demand and saved only locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
